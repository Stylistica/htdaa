Week 12 : Scalability

Assignment: Modify your design to account for scalability, and develop visual instructions (including diagrams/drawings) to facilitate making/ assembly process by the users

To modify my design for scalability, I made an iOS app Lime that can connect via bluetooth to the light timer. This assignment helped me learn how to use Xcode to develop iOS apps as well as understanding designing of apps. This app contains functionality to modify the colors of the timer, modify the brightness, set the time to any mins/hrs and reset the timer.
